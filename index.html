About Us

RRP ENTERPRISES is a diversified logistics company in India and a leading player in the domestic B2B road transportation segment. We deliver reliable, end-to-end multimodal logistics solutions designed around our customers’ needs.

At RRP ENTERPRISES, we believe logistics is more than moving cargo—it’s about keeping our customers’ businesses running smoothly. With technology-driven operations, we provide real-time visibility, scalable capacity, and customized solutions that help our clients reduce costs and improve efficiency.
